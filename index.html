FAQ

What is antimatter?
Anti-matter is a form of physical state which exists in the Universe. Some parts of the universe contain matter, others contain antimatter. Matter and antimatter are opposites in the sense that when matter and antimatter come into contact, they annihilate each other and release pure energy.
Is antimatter jelly dangerous?
Jelly is not inherently dangerous. On the otherhand, antimatter is very dangerous if you are made of matter, like us. Antimatter people, if they exist, should find the jelly very tasty and the antimatter aspect highly compatible with their digestive systems.
